# Streamline Your Phone Communications: Introducing the Aircall Node for n8n

We're excited to announce that Velocity BPA has just released a new community node that brings powerful phone system automation to n8n: the Aircall node!

## Why We Built This

Aircall is a cloud-based phone system trusted by thousands of businesses for sales and support communications. However, many teams struggle with manual data entry between their phone system and other business tools. Call logs need to be transferred to CRMs, SMS notifications need to trigger based on customer actions, and user management becomes increasingly complex as teams grow.

The n8n-nodes-aircall integration solves these challenges by connecting your Aircall phone system directly into your n8n workflows, enabling true end-to-end automation.

## Key Features

Our Aircall node gives you comprehensive control over your phone communications:

**Call Management**: Retrieve call details, search call history, and integrate call data with your CRM, analytics tools, or databases. Never manually log a call again.

**User Administration**: Automate user provisioning and management. When new team members join, automatically create their Aircall accounts and configure permissions.

**Contact Management**: Sync contacts between Aircall and your other systems. Keep customer information consistent across all platforms.

**SMS Capabilities**: Send SMS messages programmatically based on workflow triggers, perfect for appointment reminders, follow-ups, or customer notifications.

**Webhook Triggers**: React to Aircall events in real-time. When a call ends, a voicemail is received, or an SMS arrives, your n8n workflow springs into action automatically.

## Getting Started

Installing the Aircall node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-aircall


Then restart n8n, and you'll find the Aircall node ready to use in your workflow editor.

For detailed documentation and examples, visit our GitHub repository: https://github.com/Velocity-BPA/n8n-nodes-aircall
